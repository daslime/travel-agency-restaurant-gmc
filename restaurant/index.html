At Nyama Choma Haven, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Kenya. Our chefs use traditional cooking methods and locally-sourced ingredients to bring you the authentic taste of African cuisine. From our signature nyama choma to delicious coastal dishes, every meal tells a story of our culture.

Located in the heart of Nairobi, we offer a warm, welcoming atmosphere where you can enjoy the best of Kenyan hospitality.
